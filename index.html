About me

Hello! I'm Rachel

I'm a third-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at Davao del Norte State College here in Tagum City, where I was born and raised. I'm currently finishing my 3rd year and on track to move into my 4th year this coming semester.

My path to DNSC hasn't been a straight line. I previously studied at the University of Mindanao for one semester and a half before pausing for nearly three years due to financial challenges. Returning to school was a deliberate and meaningful choice, DNSC has given me a community that genuinely makes me want to show up every day.

Getting to know me takes a little patience. I tend to be reserved at first, but once I'm comfortable, you'll find someone who laughs easily and gives as much as they receive. I hold honesty and integrity close, I'm the kind of person who will always tell you the truth, even when it's easier not to.
